Whitecliff Bay

Based in Hillway, Isle of Wight, this caravan offers a picturesque view and a hop away from the local beach. Offering 2 bedrooms and sleeping up to 6 people, also allowing pets for the complete family holiday.

Owner Details

Ben Kennedy will be your contact for queries regarding the caravan, your stay and other questions. Your details are required for creating a profile on this website, allowing Ben to make contact with yourself, but if you wish to do beforehand, Ben is contactable by e-mail on ben@kennedy.com.

Getting There

The caravan is situated on the east coast of Isle Of Wight, UK. Travelling from mainland of England, ferry companies Wight Link, isle of wight ferries and Red Funnel are the main means of transport.
